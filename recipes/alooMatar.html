Recipe Name: Aloo Matar

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle for a few seconds.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until it becomes translucent.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the raw aroma disappears.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. Add the diced potatoes and green peas. Stir well to coat them with the spice mixture.
  6. Pour in the water and bring it to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover the pan, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are tender and cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or Indian bread like roti or naan. Enjoy your Aloo Matar!
